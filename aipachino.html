FAQ

What do I need to get started?

An Alpaca brokerage account, a funded balance (at least $10 recommended), and access to a web browser. You do not need trading experience.

Is there a minimum account size?

No strict minimum, but accounts under $25,000 may be subject to PDT rules for stocks. Crypto trades are not subject to PDT restrictions.

Can I paper trade before going live?

Yes. The Free Tier supports full simulation mode with real market data for practice and testing.

What exchanges are supported?

All trades are routed through Alpaca. Stocks are traded on U.S. exchanges, while crypto is routed via Alpaca Crypto LLC.

Are there any hidden fees?

No hidden fees. Platform charges are transparent and listed in the pricing section. Alpaca may charge standard regulatory fees on stock trades.

What happens if a sell order doesn't fill?

Unfilled sell orders are canceled after a timeout, and the system tracks the asset to attempt a new exit if conditions improve. No fees are charged for canceled orders.

Do I have full control over my account?

Yes. You can disconnect Aipachino, withdraw funds, or pause trading anytime from your Alpaca account settings.

Is my data safe?

Yes. We do not store your brokerage credentials. All communications use secure APIs and encryption.

How often does Aipachino trade?

Trades occur based on real-time market signals and risk parameters, usually multiple times per day depending on market conditions.

Can I customize strategies?

Yes, the Standard and Pro Tiers include strategy optimization and configuration tools.

What happens if I want to stop?

You can disconnect your account at any time and withdraw funds. There are no penalties or lock-in periods.
