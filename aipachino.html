Performance-Based Fee (Profit Share)

Best for: Clients with capital, willing to pay based on returns.

20% of realized monthly profits, after crossing a “high water mark.”

Example: If a $1,000 account grows to $1,150, your cut is 20% of the $150 gain = $30.

If it later drops to $1,130, you don’t get paid again until profits exceed $1,150.

Tip: Use Stripe, PayPal subscriptions, or crypto wallets to automate this.
