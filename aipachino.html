What is Aipachino?

Aipachino is an automated multi-asset trading system that intelligently buys and sells stocks and crypto on your behalf. It runs locally or remotely using your own Alpaca account — you maintain full control, while Aipachino works in the background.

Why Aipachino?

  • Multi-Asset Intelligence: Stocks and crypto are traded differently — Aipachino knows that. It dynamically adjusts logic based on market type, hours, and volatility patterns.
  • Real Scoring Engine: It ranks assets using momentum, volatility, and recent price action — not just static indicators.
  • Secure Execution: No cloud keys. Aipachino runs wherever you want — Raspberry Pi, server, laptop — and doesn’t require giving up custody.
  • Actionable Logs: Every trade, score, and price peak is logged to local SQLite. Great for tweaking, auditing, or building dashboards.
  • Future-Proof: AI models can be layered in later. The foundation is modular — signal intelligence, alerts, and chat interfaces are all on the roadmap.
  • Human Touch: Unlike other bots, Aipachino offers customized deployment, tailored asset selection, and real support.
  • Brand That Stands Out: Aipachino brings performance with personality. It’s your “barista bot” for passive income — always brewing.

How It Works

  • You create an Alpaca account (U.S. only).
  • You connect your API keys to Aipachino.
  • You set your rules or use defaults — then let it run.
  • You can monitor performance or let it handle everything hands-free.
