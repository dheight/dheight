Frequently Asked Questions

Who is Aipachino for?
Aipachino is designed for beginners and intermediate traders who want a stress-free, out-of-the-box trading experience for crypto and stocks. You just connect your account and provide a starting amount.
What brokerages does Aipachino support?
Currently, Aipachino connects securely to Alpaca for both stock and cryptocurrency trading within supported regions.
Are there equity or regional requirements?
Yes, Alpaca requires a minimum equity of $25,000 for stock trading in the U.S. market due to Pattern Day Trader rules. Crypto trading through Alpaca has different regional requirements; check your account eligibility accordingly.
Does Aipachino manage risk?
Yes, the system has built-in risk management features including stop losses, profit-taking triggers, and filters based on asset volatility to protect your capital.
Can I customize strategies?
Basic preferences can be configured during setup. Advanced users can modify strategy parameters if self-hosting.
Is my money safe?
Absolutely. Aipachino never holds or transfers your funds. All trades are executed via your brokerage account under your control.
How do I get started?
Simply book a setup session or view a demo. Connect your Alpaca account and provide your initial investment amount. Aipachino takes care of the rest.
