What is Aipachino?

Aipachino is a fully autonomous trading system designed for everyday investors. It connects directly to your Alpaca account and uses advanced algorithmic strategies to scan markets, evaluate opportunities, and execute trades 24/7. Built for performance, simplicity, and control, it eliminates emotional decision-making and works tirelessly to pursue profits while you focus on other things.

Whether you're trading crypto, stocks, or both, Aipachino handles portfolio management, order timing, and risk logic automatically. It’s like having your own quant team in a box.
